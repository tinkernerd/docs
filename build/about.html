About Nick!

Hey there! I'm Nick, better known online as The Tinkering Nerd. Welcome to my Digital Notebook, 2nd Brain, and Digital Garden — a space where ideas sprout, evolve, and occasionally get pruned. Some notes here might be works in progress, others might stay polished, and a few may leave you scratching your head. Curious? Head over to the to explore the gems and oddities.

A quick heads-up: I might have ADHD, so expect a delightful mix of topics as I chase inspiration wherever it leads. Buckle up and enjoy the ride!

Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Southwestern Michigan College . My interests span far and wide, so you’ll find notes here on programming (Python, PowerShell, and beyond), Docker, Raspberry Pi projects, 3D printing, Networking, Aviation, Recipes, GitHub tips, and more. There's something for everyone!
